World Of Crazy Heroes

— 2017 —


World Of Crazy Heroes is a fun, third person, multiplayer game, in which you
fight as a hero you select. You can do it either in a team or alone.

To replicate a complex thing like this wingsuit was a very interesting challenge to me.
The player calculates the flight mechanics local,
while passing highly simplified data to the server to save performance.

INFO
  • Game Engine: UE4 (Blueprint)
  • Target group: People who like PVP and friends who want to have fun together

  • Code written by me
  • Characters designed in Fuse CC by me
  • 3D assets: UE4 Marketplace
