World of Crazy Heroes

— 2017 —


World of Crazy Heroes is a fun, third person, multiplayer game, in which you are
fighting as a hero you select. You can do it either in a team or alone.

Replicating a complex thing like this wingsuit was a very interesting challenge to me.
The player calculates the flight mechanics local,
while passing highly simplified data to the server to save performance.

INFO
  • Game Engine: UE4 (Blueprint)
  • Target group: 16+
  • characters designed in Fuse CC, meshes aren't created by me, programing by me
